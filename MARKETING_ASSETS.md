## 📋 Press Kit

### Product Description (Short)
AI-powered content repurposing tool that transforms blog posts into platform-optimized social media content for Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram. Built with Next.js, Appwrite, and Stripe. Privacy-first with local AI support via LM Studio.

### Product Description (Long)
Content Repurposing Engine is a SaaS micro-tool designed for content creators, marketers, and solopreneurs who publish long-form content but struggle to repurpose it across social media platforms. Users paste their blog post into the engine, and within seconds receive 4 optimized posts — one tailored for each major platform (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram). Each generated post respects character limits, platform conventions, audience expectations, and includes relevant hashtags. The tool supports both cloud-based AI processing and local LM Studio integration for privacy-conscious users. Pricing starts at $9/month with free trial access.

### Key Differentiators
1. **Platform-Specific Optimization** — Not just generic posts; each output is tailored to its platform's audience and format
2. **Privacy-First Architecture** — Option to process content locally via LM Studio (no data leaves your machine)
3. **Affordable Pricing** — Starting at $9/month, significantly cheaper than enterprise content tools
4. **Instant Results** — Generation completes in under 10 seconds
5. **Open Source Core** — Repository available on GitHub for transparency
